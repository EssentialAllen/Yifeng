Our theater and auditorium seating combines ergonomic principles with precise material design to achieve optimal acoustic effects and perfectly reflect your venue's architectural style.

Retractable Stands & Stage

Maximize your venue's flexibility with our high-utilization retractable stands, which form a staircase profile when in use and a compact box shape when retracted. They feature a synchronous telescoping device to effectively avoid deviation or jamming during operation.

The base utilizes double ball-bearing PU wheels that withstand enormous force without damaging wooden floors, and the entire system can be safely operated via wire or wireless remote control.

Recent Projects

Featured Project

PNE Amphitheatre Seating Installation

100 percent completed

We are leading the fabrication and installation of a large-scale seating system for the PNE Amphitheatre, delivering 5,738 seats across fixed and removable configurations. The project involves complex coordination with the general contractor, design team, and site trades, working within tight timelines and evolving site conditions.

Our scope includes precision layout, custom fabrication, and on-site installation across a challenging sloped and radiused structure. A key focus is ensuring durability, accessibility compliance, and flexibility for event-based reconfiguration, particularly within the removable seating areas.

Through careful planning, adaptive installation strategies, and close on-site coordination, the project is progressing efficiently while maintaining quality and performance standards.
